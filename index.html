Branding

What the company offers

The comapny (SEEDA) provides engineering and project management services to clients in oil, gas, mining, petrochemical, pipeline, infrastructure, power generation and water treatment projects.

The potential customers

Potential customers include investors, energy and infrastructure operators, as well as engineering, procurement and construction organizations that are seeking experts in order to receive advice in these areas.

Why this comapny might entice someone to purchase their service

The company is run by a diverse team of experts who can provide new perspectives on the client's projects, reducing environmental footprints and lowering costs. In addition to their associates, SEEDA also has access to many engineers who are based in Western Canada.
